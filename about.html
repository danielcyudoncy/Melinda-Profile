Melinda Akinlami

Is a seasoned and versatile Broadcaster with over 20 years of experience cutting across News/Programme Presentation,Production, Lifestyle, Culture and Art Reportage.

She is the Supervising Producer and Presenter of an Art and programme on Channels Television called Arthouse which is a 30 minute show that reveals the Beauty, Art, Culture and Creativity that abound in Nigeria, Africa and the World.

A Professional Art Critique, Dealer/Marketer and Consultant. She established ONOME ART CONNECT and GALLERY due to her passion and dedication for the Visual Art Industry. The goal is to connect potential art buyers with top-notch artworks of the leading and renowned Nigerian and international artists.

The belief that art is high end and for a select few is a myth, Everyone can afford it, and it's not entirely a luxury. Our mission is to fix those who desire to acquire a quality art piece with exceptional works of art that match their budgets.

A Christian, Wife and Mother who is also passionate about issues relating to women and children is presently running a foundation to offer support for those who have passed through abusive situations.
